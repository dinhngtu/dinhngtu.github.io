About me

I'm Dinh Ngoc Tu, a PhD student belonging to the SEPIA research team at the Institut de Recherche en Informatique de Toulouse (University of Toulouse, France) under the tutelages of Dr. Georges Da Costa and Pr. Daniel Hagimont. My research focuses on operating systems and virtualization.

I was previously a System Engineer at CMC System Integration Hanoi (now CMC Technology & Solution). I obtained my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from the University of Science and Technology of Hanoi (aka. Vietnam–France University).
